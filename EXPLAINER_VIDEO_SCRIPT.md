## PRODUCTION SPECIFICATIONS

### Video Format
- **Resolution**: 1920 x 1080 (1080p HD)
- **Frame Rate**: 30 fps (smooth on all devices)
- **Codec**: H.264 (universal compatibility)
- **Duration**: 120 seconds (fits YouTube pre-roll)

### Audio
- **Voiceover**: Clear, professional, slight Indian accent (relatable)
- **Pacing**: Medium-fast (120 words per minute, matching logistics industry energy)
- **Music**: Royalty-free startup/tech vibe (Epidemic Sound or similar)
- **Sound Effects**: Subtle (whoosh on data entry, satisfying ding on completion)
- **Audio Mix**: Voiceover: 60%, Music: 30%, SFX: 10%

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Navy Blue (#0f3a7d)
- **Accent**: Orange (#f97316)
- **Success**: Green (#22c55e)
- **Background**: White/Light Gray (#f8f9fa)
- **Text**: Dark Gray (#1a1a1a)

### Typography
- **Font**: Inter or similar (modern, clean, professional)
- **Heading Size**: 32px
- **Body Text**: 16px
- **All caps for emphasis**: Sparingly

### Animation Style
- **Tone**: Modern, smooth, not cartoonish
- **Transitions**: 0.3-0.5s duration, easing functions
- **Emphasis**: Green checkmarks, glows, scale effects
- **Pace**: Matches voiceover speed (sync audio and visuals)
